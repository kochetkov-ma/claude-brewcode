# Anti-Slop Prompt Prefixes

Prepend the matching variant to every user prompt before sending to the API. These constraints reduce common AI image artifacts.

---

## photo

```
CRITICAL CONSTRAINTS — Physically accurate photography:
- Real-world lighting physics: single coherent light source, correct shadow direction and softness
- Correct human anatomy: exactly 5 fingers per hand, proper proportions, natural skin texture
- Real materials: fabric wrinkles follow gravity, metal reflects environment, glass refracts
- No AI artifacts: no glow halos, no plastic skin, no floating objects, no impossible geometry
- No procedural patterns: no repeating tile textures, no fractal noise surfaces
- Camera-realistic: proper depth of field, chromatic aberration at edges, subtle lens distortion
```

---

## illustration

```
CRITICAL CONSTRAINTS — Professional illustration quality:
- Clean, intentional line work: consistent stroke weight, no wobbly AI artifacts
- Proper color theory: harmonious palette, intentional contrast, no random saturation spikes
- Hand-drawn quality: slight organic imperfections welcome, NO perfect symmetry
- Correct perspective: single vanishing point or intentional isometric, never mixed
- No AI smoothing: preserve texture and grain appropriate to medium
- Functional elements: doors have handles, cups have bases, chairs have four legs
```

---

## art

```
CRITICAL CONSTRAINTS — Consistent artistic medium:
- Single coherent medium: oil paint OR watercolor OR digital paint, never mixed unintentionally
- Intentional composition: rule of thirds or golden ratio, clear focal point
- No AI smoothing: preserve brushstroke texture, canvas grain, paper tooth
- Consistent lighting: one light source direction throughout the piece
- No impossible physics: gravity works, reflections are accurate, shadows consistent
- Color coherence: unified color temperature, no random neon in a muted palette
```
